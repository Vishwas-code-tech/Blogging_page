A night dark story



The night was darker than usual, with thick clouds hiding the moon and stars, casting the world into a shadowy silence. The wind whispered through the trees, carrying an eerie chill that crept under the skin. Somewhere in the distance, a lone owl hooted, breaking the heavy stillness. In the dim glow of a flickering streetlight, a figure moved slowly down the empty road, footsteps echoing like distant thunder. Every sound seemed louder, every shadow seemed alive. It was the kind of night where secrets hid in the darkness and fear walked hand in hand with silence.
