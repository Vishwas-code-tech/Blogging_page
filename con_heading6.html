A newspaper heading plays a powerful role in capturing the reader’s attention and setting the tone for the story that follows. It is the first thing people see, often deciding whether they will read the article or skip it. A strong heading is clear, concise, and informative, giving readers a quick idea of the main topic or event. It should spark curiosity while staying true to the facts, avoiding exaggeration or confusion. In print and digital media alike, a good headline helps organize information, guides the reader through the content, and reflects the professionalism of the publication. Whether it's breaking news, a feature story, or an editorial, the heading acts like a gateway—pulling readers in and preparing them for the information that lies ahead.
